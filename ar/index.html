TickTock Tales is a free educational game that helps children aged 5–9 learn to tell time on an analog clock. With three difficulty levels, kids start with whole hours and progress to reading any minute on the clock face.

Our fun clock reading practice includes instant feedback, a streak system, and collectible badges to keep kids motivated. The free play mode lets children drag the clock hands to explore time at their own pace.

Available in English, French, Spanish and Arabic — perfect for bilingual families, international schools, and homeschooling parents worldwide.
